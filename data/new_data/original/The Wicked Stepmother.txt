Once upon a time, there was a young girl named Cinderella who lived with her stepmother and stepsisters. Her stepmother treated her very poorly and made her do all the household chores. One day, the king of the land announced a ball, and Cinderella's stepsisters were invited. Cinderella wished to go too, but her stepmother refused and made her stay at home. However, with the help of her fairy godmother, Cinderella was able to attend the ball and even catch the prince's attention. The prince fell in love with her, and they got married. The wicked stepmother learned a valuable lesson: treating others poorly will eventually lead to one's downfall.
