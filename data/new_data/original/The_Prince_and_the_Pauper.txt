Once upon a time, a prince who was curious about the life of common people decided to dress as a pauper and wander the streets. He met a poor boy who was also curious about the life of royalty and the two swapped places. The prince struggled to survive in poverty while the pauper lived a life of luxury. Eventually, the prince's father found out what had happened and the boys were reunited. The prince learned a valuable lesson: it's important to understand and appreciate the lives of people from all walks of life.
