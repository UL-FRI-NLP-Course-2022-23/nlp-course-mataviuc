It happened that Fox caught Fox tail in a trap, and in struggling to release Fox lost all of its tail but the stump. At first Fox was ashamed to show Fox among Fox fellow foxes . But at last Fox determined to put a bolder face upon Fox misfortune , and summoned their to a general meeting to consider a proposal which Fox had to place before their . When their had assembled together Fox proposed that their should all do away with their tails . Fox pointed out how inconvenient tail was when their were pursued by their enemies , the dogs ; how much its tail was in the way when their desired to sit down and hold a friendly conversation with one another . Fox failed to see any advantage in carrying about such a useless encumbrance . “ That is all very well , ” said one of  older foxes ; “ but one of  older foxes do not think Fox would have recommended their to dispense with their chief ornament if Fox had not happened to lose tail Fox . ” Distrust interested advice .
