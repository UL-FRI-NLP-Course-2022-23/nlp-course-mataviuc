Hart was once drinking from a pool and admiring the noble figure Hart made there. “ Ah, ” said Hart , “ where can you see such noble horns as such noble horns as these , with such antlers , with such antlers ! Hart wish Hart had legs more worthy to bear such  noble crown ; it is a pity legs more worthy to bear such  noble crown are so slim and slight . ” At that moment Hunter approached and sent an arrow whistling after Hart . Away bounded Hart , and soon , by the aid of Hart nimble legs , was nearly out of sight of Hunter ; but not noticing where Hart was going , Hart passed under some trees with branches growing low down in which Hart antlers were caught , so that Hunter had time to come up . “ Alas ! alas ! ” cried Hart : “ We often despise what is most useful to We . ”
