A Lion used to prowl about field. Many a time A Lion tried to attack Four Oxen; but whenever A Lion came near Four Oxen turned Four Oxen tails to one another , so that whichever way A Lion approached Four Oxen A Lion was met by the horns of one of Four Oxen . At last , however , Four Oxen fell a - quarrelling among Four Oxen , and each went off to pasture alone in a separate corner of field . Then A Lion attacked Four Oxen one by one and soon made an end of Four Oxen . United we stand , divided we fall .
